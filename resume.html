Research Interests

My research interest is in Human-Computer Interaction, where I explore the potential of technology:

  • To promote conscientious classroom discussions through tools like the ThoughtSwap system, which supplements classroom pedagogy to enhance discourse.
  • To empower marginalized communities by addressing power dynamics through technology.

My research bridges multiple academic disciplines, applying interdisciplinary methods to solve problems as a computer scientist.
